Alex Russo

Alex Russo is the protagonist of the Disney show, "Wizard of Waverly Place" that ran during 2007 to 2012. Many kids and teens during the time, including myself, loved the show and could relate to Alex Russo! Throughout the show, she displayed her humorous, apathetic, sometimes loving traits, and many more. She is an influential person to those (aka me) who watched her show.
