About Alex Russo

Alexandra Margarita "Alex" Russo is the main protagonist of Wizards of Waverly Place. She is the only daughter of Jerry and Theresa Russo, the younger sister of Justin and the older sister of Max. In the finale season, she is the Family Wizard after winning the Family Wizard Competition. She is also in a long-term relationship with werewolf Mason Greybeck.

At one point, Alex was the most powerful Wizard in the world! Spells and Magic-related Powers that Alex showcased throughout the show were: Cloning, Rewinding Time, Controlling Others, Truth Spell, Telekinesis, Levitation, Molecular Manipulation, Teleportation, Enchantment, etc.

Her other abilities that don't involve spells or anything supernatural includes Pickpocketing, Stealthiness, and basic Combat.

Alex is not overly studious nor is she an academically gifted student. Rather, she relies more on her street-smarts than academics. She also has a tedency to be very lazy. However, as she matured, she started to become more studious in both magic and mortal lessons, managing to beat Justin in the Family Wizard Competition. She's very manipulative, and known to be a mischievious troublemaker. One of her flaws is her own reliance on magic and her laziness, which often results in magical troubles, as well her lack of disregard for rules.
Despite her frequent unkindness and pranks, Alex is far from heartless. She deeply cares for Harper, going so far as to reveal her secret to her in "Harper Knows" out of guilt for lying, and loves her brothers dearly despite their constant rivalry, especially with Justin. Throughout the show, you can see Alex grow as she frequents the feeling of remorse and guilt and has apologized and taken responsibility for her actions.
