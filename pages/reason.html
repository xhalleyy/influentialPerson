Why is Alex Russo INFLUENTIAL?

As a kid and a teen, I thoroughly enjoyed Alex Russo's character and overall development. Throughout the show, we see her mature and since I was also growing, it felt like I was maturing as well. Alex is a very relatable teen, who doesn't excel in many subjects, is very lazy, and envies her siblings. Her emotions are very realistic. Though she seems like a delinquient, she is a very kind-hearted and empathetic despite her apathetic front. I also loved her humor and sarcasticness. It feels as though her character somewhat shaped my own humor.
