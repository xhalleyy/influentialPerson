#BOHEMIAN #CHIC

#CASUAL #TRENDY

Alex Russo's style was very trendy at the time. Typically, when you look back at 2000s and 2010s fashion, you'd question the fashion sense back then. However, even with the tons of layering Alex wore, it somehow seems so put together. Typically, she tends to dress down, but even her casual clothes seem very well put together. Everything from her pajamas to dresses to shoes to accessories seemed very chic on her. Throughout the seasons, he style varied but she looked great nonetheless. She often wore sweaters/ long sleeves with elaborate shirts. For a bit, she seemed obsessed with scarves. Adding multi-colored accessories with it added a feminine and sophisticated flair to her wardrobe.

Other than clothes, I feel like Alex's hair was always perfect. It felt like her hair was a statement piece. She most often had perfect wavy curls. Later into the show, she even got highlights!
